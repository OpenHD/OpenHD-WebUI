What Makes OpenHD so great

Explore unlimited possibilities

OpenHD is an exceptionally versatile system, adaptable to a wide array of platforms and diverse hardware configurations. Tailored with a strong focus on the FPV hobbyist community, OpenHD remains responsive to valuable feedback from its extensive user base; its remarkable modularity and user-friendly design render OpenHD an invaluable solution across various applications, including education, industry, and the everyday use of FPV enthusiasts.

Long Range

OpenHD's groundbreaking long-range system empowers users to achieve extraordinary communication distances. Distances exceeding 50 kilometers are realistically achievable within optimal settings.

📷

Cameras

OpenHD offers support for a vast array of cameras and image sensors. It enables high-definition video, making it an essential component for exploration or scenarios that ask for exceptional clarity.

Custom Hardware

With its declared hardware-agnostic nature, OpenHD integrates seamlessly with a diverse range of bespoke hardware configurations, including specialized flight control systems and custom ground stations.
